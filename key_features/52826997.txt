This property is sold with no onward chain.
Spring Lodge is situated in the sought after village of Hatcliffe, one of the prettiest villages in the Lincolnshire Wolds.
The property enjoys an elevated plot with views across its own pond and open fields beyond.
There is a self contained one bedroom annexe, with a bathroom and a kitchen.
The rear garden is beautifully maintained with a paved seating area surrounded by well stocked flower beds and a picturesque pond with open fields beyond.
The property also benefits from solar thermal heating via glass tiles on the annexe roof.
