Jubilee Cottage is set in a large plot with a gravel barbeque area, various lawns, a pond and backs on to open fields with two timber stables and a tack room.
Further amenities can be found in the market town of Louth approximately 4.7 miles away with its excellent range of shops and facilities.
Jubilee Cottage enjoys vaulted ceilings, exposed beams and is awash with natural light.
The property is approximately 5.2 miles from the King Edward VII Grammar School in Louth, rated 'Outstanding' in its last Ofsted report.
Covenham Reservoir, approximately 3.4 miles away, is a popular nature and bird watching venue as well as a water sports facility.
Kenwick is six miles away and offers an equestrian centre as well as a golf club and Spa.
