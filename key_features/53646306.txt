Approximately 5.3 away lies Louth which offers a range of shops, restaurants and a choice of schooling, including the Ofsted 'outstanding' King Edward VI Grammar School.
Brambledown is situated approximately six miles from the Saltfleetby Theddlethorpe Dunes, a statutory National Nature Reserve with many walking trails.
There are two excellent golf clubs nearby with Kenwick Park (approx. Four miles) & Louth Golf club (approx. 5.6 miles).
The generous gardens have been beautifully maintained and cleverly planted creating an open and private space with a paved barbeque terrace, ornamental pond and seating areas.
The triple aspect sitting room has a brick fireplace with oak mantle and double doors opening in to the garden room which enjoys wrap around garden views and double doors opening on to the garden.
