Skirbeck Barn lies approximately 1.4 miles West of Barkston village, an idyllic rural village enjoying a traditional country pub, garage shop, village hall, church and primary school
Nearby schooling includes the Kings Grammar School and the Kesteven and Grantham Girls' School at Grantham, both of which are Ofsted 'Outstanding' and Carre's Grammar School at Sleaford
Grantham train station is approximately 4 miles away and offers a direct rail service to London (Kings Cross from 70 minutes)
Skirbeck Barn is an attractive four bedroom stone Barn Conversion laid out over two floors offering an excellent entertaining space
The garden back on to open fields enjoys far reaching views all around
The gated courtyard has hard standing for multiple vehicles and access to the triple carport and workshop/garage
