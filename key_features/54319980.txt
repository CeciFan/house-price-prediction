The highly sought-after picturesque conservation village of Brattleby lies approximately five miles north of the Cathedral City of Lincoln
The nearby village of Scampton has a Ofsted 'Good' primary school and the Dambusters Inn
The position of Glebe Farm allows far-reaching and fabulously beautiful views over the Trent Valley
The impressive 25' triple aspect reception room with vaulted ceilings and exposed wooden beams has a shower room and its own entrance which could potentially suit multi generation living
Outside the property is approached via an imposing tarmac driveway which leads to a large gravel driveway and turning circle complete with planted central island and garaging
The grounds extend to about 8.5 acres and have a terrace area, woodland and wild flowers, pond, working well, flower and kitchen garden and a large greenhouse
